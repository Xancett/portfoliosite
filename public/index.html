About me

Experienced software developer with 14 years of experience working with computers. Intrinsically motivated for self-education and known for taking on complex problems that require creative solutions. I left highschool with experience in Visual Basic, C++ and Java and spent years afterwards working on various projects. I started a company to create video games and have since released a mobile puzzle game for iOS and Android with over 30 thousand levels. I wrote all of the code in the game including a custom level creator and solver designed to create unique and interesting levels. I've recently been building web apps using a variety of languages and frameworks.
